Scenario Description

Do people prefer to tweet more on weekend or week day? During day time or at night? When do people write longer tweets? Is there any city where citizens are always brief when tweeting? Having those questions in mind, we start our investigation on people's tweeting behaviors. In this scenario, we focus on the feature 'created_at' which is the posting time for each tweet and length and word count for 'text'.

After converting the time, there is a pattern that people have less time on tweeting in the morning, before lunch time;  but tweet more at night, particularly before bed time(around midnight).

Chart Analysis:
