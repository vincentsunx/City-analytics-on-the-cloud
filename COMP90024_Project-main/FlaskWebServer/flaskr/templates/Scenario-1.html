Senario 1:

The pandemic in Australia is being well controlled, however,  it continues to impact many other countries in the world.  The primary task recently is to speed up vaccination in Australia. Above all, we want to know people's attention on Covid-19 by searching the amount of coronavirus related tweets - those tweets include relative topics within text - in different cities. And then comparing people's attention with the real Covid-19 situation in different areas to find out if any relation exists.

Chart Analysis:
This chart illustrates the total number of tweets mentioned COVID-19. Actually, in this figure, we can see Sydney and Melbourne have the most number of COVID-19 twitter. But one thing should be noticed that these two areas have more population than the others. Thus, we calculated all 5 areas amount and let it divide by 1000 to get the average situation on Chart 2 on the Right.

Chart Analysis:
This chart we illustrate the number of tweets mentioned COVID-19 per thousand tweets. Since these five areas have different populations, I calculated the average situation for analysis. Based on these charts, we could conclude that people in Brisbane and Perth pay less attention on COVID-19 recently, and people in Sydney usually care about COVID-19.

Chart Analysis (Infection Rate):
Based on this chart, we could conclude that: the people in Melbourne has highest infection rate and Brisbane, Perth and Adelaide has the lower infection rate. Also, the infection rate in Melbourne is almost 10 times of these three cities' infection rate.

Chart Analysis (Vaccine Rate):
In this graph, we show the vaccination rates in various regions. According to the graph, we can find that Perth has the highest vaccination rate up to 2.42 per 1000 people. Although according to Chart2 we know that Melbourne had the largest number of infections at the time, but Their vaccination rates are mediocre.

Scenario Analysis:

Melbourne, Sydney and Adelaide have more tweets related to Covid-19 and they are indeed the top three capital cities having the highest infection rate. The vaccination complet rate doesn’t differ much between each city, but those three areas still have a slightly higher rate than others. Maybe citizens in those areas are more willing to use the vaccine to protect themselves and the government’s arrangement is more active. As a result, people’s attention to Covid-19 does related to the real Covid-19 situation.However, we all know that such a high infection in Melbourne is mostly caused by the international flight. Thus we cannot simply conclude there is any infrastructure problem in Greater Melbourne.
